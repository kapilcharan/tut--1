Solar Dryer

A solar dryer is an innovative and eco-friendly appliance designed to harness the power of the sun for efficient and natural drying of various materials, including fruits, vegetables, herbs, clothes, and more. It operates by converting solar energy into heat, creating a controlled environment that gently removes moisture from items, preserving their freshness and quality while reducing energy costs and environmental impact.
